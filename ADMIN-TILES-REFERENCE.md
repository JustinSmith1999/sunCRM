# Admin Dashboard - Complete Tile Reference

Your admin page now has 23 management tiles organized in a 5-column grid. Here's everything you can manage:

## Core Administration
1. **User Mappings** - Map Supabase users to Salesforce users
2. **User Management** - Create, edit, and manage user accounts
3. **HR Console** - Manage employee records, departments, and HR data
4. **System Settings** - Configure system-wide preferences, security, notifications, and integrations

## Salesforce & Integration
5. **Salesforce Sync** - Bidirectional sync between Supabase and Salesforce
6. **Salesforce Requests** - Track and manage Salesforce enhancement requests
7. **Engineering Requests** - Submit and track technical requests

## Lead Generation & Forms
8. **Web-to-Lead Forms** - Create and manage web forms for lead capture
9. **Channel Partners** - Manage partner relationships, commissions, and portal access
10. **Automation Flows** - Build automated workflows and triggers

## Business Management
11. **Leads Management** - View and manage all leads
12. **Deals Pipeline** - Kanban board for sales opportunities
13. **Accounts** - Customer and company account management
14. **Support Cases** - Customer support ticket management
15. **Contacts Console** - Comprehensive contact management

## Marketing & Sales
16. **Marketing Campaigns** - Plan and track marketing campaigns
17. **Product Catalog** - Manage products, pricing, and inventory

## Analytics & Reporting
18. **Dashboard Builder** - Create custom dashboards
19. **Reports & Analytics** - View reports and analytics
20. **Knowledge Base** - Internal documentation and help articles

## Finance & Operations
21. **Finance Dashboard** - Financial metrics and reporting
22. **Company Equipment** - Track company assets and equipment
23. **Software Subscriptions** - Manage software licenses and subscriptions

## How to Access

1. Click on "Administration" in the sidebar
2. Click any tile to navigate to that management console
3. All tiles use consistent color coding and icons for easy identification

## Layout

The admin page displays tiles in a responsive grid:
- **Mobile**: 2 columns
- **Tablet**: 4 columns
- **Desktop**: 5 columns

Each tile shows:
- A colored icon representing the function
- The tile label describing what it manages
- Hover effects for visual feedback
